# Todo Tracker

IssuePit includes a personal **Todo Tracker** that helps you manage day-to-day tasks across all projects in one place.

---

## Overview

The Todo Tracker is accessible via the **Todos** link in the sidebar. It provides:

- A **Board view** — Kanban-style columns (Backlog, Todo, In Progress, Done)
- A **Weekly Calendar view** — todos laid out by day of the week with drag-and-drop scheduling

---

## Creating a Todo

1. Navigate to **Todos** in the sidebar.
2. Click **New Todo**.
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title** — a short description
   - **Due Date** *(optional)* — assign a day
   - **Priority** — Low, Medium, or High
4. Click **Save**.

You can also create a todo directly from a calendar day slot by clicking on any empty cell in the weekly calendar.

---

## Board View

The board view shows todos organised into columns by status. Drag and drop cards between columns to update their status instantly.

---

## Weekly Calendar View

Switch to the **Calendar** tab to see a week-at-a-glance layout. Todos with a due date appear on the corresponding day. Drag a card to a different day to reschedule it.

Use the navigation arrows to move between weeks.

---

## iCal Subscription

You can subscribe to your todos from any calendar application (Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.) using the iCal feed:

1. Go to **Todos**.
2. Click the **Subscribe** (calendar icon) button.
3. Copy the iCal URL and paste it into your calendar application as a new subscribed calendar.

The feed updates automatically as you create, reschedule, or complete todos.

---

## MCP Support

The Todo Tracker exposes tools via the built-in **IssuePit MCP server**, so AI agents can read and create todos on your behalf. No extra configuration is needed — the MCP server is automatically linked to every agent mode.
